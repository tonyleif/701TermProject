About

Data DVR allows you to see information about a play as you stream it or watch it on DVR. Currently, you can watch a game real time or on-demand. You can get data real time, but you can't really get it on demand. If you were to look up play by play information about a game, you would more than likely see the outcome of the game, thus ruining the experience of watching it on demand.

Currently, this is only implemented for the NFL and the 2017 regular season. In the future, I plan to add all available seasons and, perhaps, other sports as well.

Eventually, I'd like to add the use case of importing your fantasy teams and seeing the scores of your games but ONLY for the games and plays you've watched so far.
